## About


I am an AI Research Engineer at [KRAFTON AI](https://www.krafton.ai/), where I work on in-game embodied agents for real-time human-AI collaboration. My research focuses on evaluating and aligning co-playable agents by grounding model behavior in gameplay trajectories, dialogue, and player preference signals. I am particularly interested in building evaluation and data-generation pipelines that make agent behavior more measurable, scalable, and aligned with user experience.
